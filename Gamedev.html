IoT -Based Virtual Reality Comforting Movement Design For Healing Rehabilitation

This project affords a pioneering technique to muscle healing that mixes virtual reality and rehabilitation therapy. Traditional rehabilitation techniques frequently lack engagement and real-time comments, leading to suboptimal effects. Our machine makes use of a custom developed virtual reality game designed for muscle healing, in which users engage using body movements captured by IoT sensors. These sensors gather information on muscle pastime and range of movement, allowing the machine to provide personalized feedback and adapt the sport mechanics as a result.

Through gamification strategies including rewards and challenges, customers are motivated to actively participate in their rehabilitation manner. Additionally, the system includes functions for far flung monitoring, allowing healthcare professionals to music affected person progress and adjust protocols as wished. Through user testing, we demonstrate the feasibility and effectiveness of our method, which could revolutionize muscle rehabilitation by making it more engaging, powerful, and handy.
